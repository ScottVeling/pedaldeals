How does this work?

Hey, I'm Velin. I ride bikes in the Netherlands and I got tired of manually checking 10+ shops every day for price drops. So I wrote a bot that does it for me — it crawls through Mantel, Bike24, Canyon, Wiggle, Futurumshop and a bunch of other stores, compares current prices to what they used to cost, and flags anything with a solid discount.

I still go through the results myself before anything ends up on the site. The bot finds the deals, but I decide what's actually worth showing. Not everything that's "on sale" is a good deal — some shops just inflate the original price. If it's here, it's because I'd genuinely consider buying it.

If the bot missed something or you know of a deal, feel free to reach out.

Transparency: this site shows a few small ads to cover hosting costs. I don't get paid by any of these shops, and none of the links are affiliate links.
